What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. It enables the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time.

How do we use cookies?

This website uses cookies to improve your experience. We use the following types of cookies based on the consent you provide:

  • Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function. The cookie consent banner itself uses a cookie to remember your privacy preferences.
  • Analytical Cookies: With your consent, we use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. This helps us understand traffic and improve the site. The cookies collect information in a way that does not directly identify anyone.
  • Advertising Cookies: With your consent, these cookies (managed through Google's services) may be used to provide personalization and improve the relevance of advertising you may see.
